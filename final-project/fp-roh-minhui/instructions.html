Instructions

  1. The goal of this game is to obtain sum of 15 horizontally, vertically and diagonally all at the same time!
  2. Use numbers from 1 to 9 only once to complete the table.
  3. Choose a number by clicking on it.
  4. Undo your choice by clicking on the chosen number again.
  5. Click on "New Game" to restart and "Solution" to see one of the solutions. (Your answer might be different from the solution but that doesn't mean you're wrong - there are many possible answers.)
  6. Enjoy!
