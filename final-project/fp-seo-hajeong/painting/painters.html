Claude Monet

This painting is called the Sunrise, painted in 1872. It is depicting a Le Havre port landscape

He is the founder of the artistic movement called the impressionism.

Impressionism is characterized by relatively small, thin, yet visible bruhs storkes. It emphasizes the accurate depiction of light

Claude Monet is my favourite painter, and I often try to do impressionism in my paintings




Jackson Pollock

This painting is called the Number 1A, painted in 1948.

Pollock is best known for what is called "poured" paintings

He uses the process of dripping paint onto a flat canvas at different times. He takes weeks before he finishes a canvas.

He is known fot these abstract paintings.




Georgia O'Keeffee

This painting is called the Purple Leaves, painted in 1922.

O'Keefee was a prominent artist who lived in New York

He was interested in nature. He isolated the plant leaves from their natural settings and magnified the mso that they would take the entire perimeter of the canvas.

He also tried to do abstraction with them.
