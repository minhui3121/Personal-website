Hobby 2 - Playing Videogames

  • In these days, playing videogame is a widely shared hobby between many people. They play using their computer, smartphone or games console. Personally, I play video games on my computer and smartphone. The game that I play the most is called League of Legends. It's a 5 vs 5 multiplayer online battle game and the goal is to distroy opponents' towers. More about this game can be found in the link below.
    More about league of legends

  • I like to play videogames as my hobby mainly because it's fun to play. Making strategies and thinking of how can I improve my skill is just something I like to do. Also, since most videogames allow several people to play at the same time, it's a good way to hangout with friends without actually meeting each others. Finally, playing videogame sometimes helps me to refresh myself when I'm having hard time.

  • Although I really enjoy playing videogames, it is a hobby that I need to be careful of. Excessive playing can be bad for my eyes and wrists, farthermore, my health in general. I also need to ensure that I've finished all my work before I play since I might forget to do something and it is challenging to concentrate right after playing videogames.
