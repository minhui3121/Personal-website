Hobby 1 - Reading Webtoons

  • Webtoons are series of digital comics. We can read them by smartphones or computers using internet portal. Originated from South Korea, it is now being translated into many different languages. There are many types of genres, starting from comedy to action, romance, or even sports. Their length also varies. Some of them are composed of less than 50 episodes while there are even ones with over 1000 episodes. The link provided below leads to one of the famous webtoon portal.
    Webtoon

  • I read Webtoons using an application from the company "Naver". Most of the pieces are being uploaded once a week. My favorite comic is called "Wind breaker". It talks about teenagers who grew up in different environment but had same passion on bicking. They somehow made a bicycle crew together and pursue their goal into professional level. I read webtoons mainly for fun, but some of them also give me useful life advices. They also make me relaxed by dragging me into the world of fictions.
